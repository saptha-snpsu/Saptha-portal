About SAPTHA

A Smart Campus platform proudly designed & developed by the CSE Department, 2024 Batch.

Our Mission

To create a centralized digital platform that integrates all campus resources, enabling seamless learning by providing students with instant access to notes, textbooks, announcements, and departmental updates in one place.

Our Vision

To establish a single, reliable, and authoritative portal that replaces scattered communication channels, ensuring coordinators can publish verified information while students can easily access accurate and up-to-date resources whenever needed.
